Our Mission

Qur'aan & Sunnah Society

A community organisation dedicated to the propagation of authentic Islamic knowledge based purely upon the Book of Allah and the authentic Sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ. We operate the local Masjid in Mont Fleuri, offering daily congregational prayers, educational programmes, and community support across Mahé.
